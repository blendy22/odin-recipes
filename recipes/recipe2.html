Wild Mushroom Rissoto

Ingredients

  • 160g rice
  • 500ml stock
  • Olive oil
  • Splash of white wine
  • 2 cloves garlic finely chopped
  • 1 shallot onion finely chopped
  • Some Thyme
  • 200g wild mushrooms chopped/sliced to cook evenly

Instructions

Prep: 5 mins Cooking: 15 mins


Pan for risotto (takes 17 minutes)

  1. Cook oil and onions in a pan
  2. Add the rice to the pan
  3. After a few minutes add white wine
  4. Add about 100ml of stock
  5. A few minutes later add another 100ml stock
  6. Add some thyme

Pan for mushrooms (takes 5 minutes)

  1. Heat oil in a hot pan
  2. Add shallot onion and garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add wild mushrooms to the pan
  4. Cook for 5 minutes and add thyme and mix well
  5. Add mushrooms to risotto pan
